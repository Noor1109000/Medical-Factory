About The Medical Factory

Our Mission

Saving lives in golden hour and improving health of the world’s poorest, needy and most vulnerable people by closing the gap through modern innovation between knowledge and action in public health services.

Our Vision

A world where everyone has the opportunity not only for timely treatment but also active, healthy and dignified life.

Why need this service?

Emergency Response

Over billion people lack access to reliable emergency response during medical emergencies, and the people who get the response need to deal with delay and unusual costing. During emergency every single second plays a very important role a delay in any of the emergency response could lead to the death. Learn more.

Indian healthcare and medical industry

In spite of the fact that the Indian healthcare and medical industry is rapidly growing, a noticeable percentage The overall Indian healthcare market today is worth US$ 100 billion but India still suffers from poor and low standards of these services, owing to people are still not connected and updated with these advancements.

Blood Deficit

India has a blood deficit between 30% to 35% every year with few states as high as 50% to 60%. Despite being a country with a population of 1.2 billion, it is unreasonable to say we can’t meet the requirement.

Position Rank of India

India ranks at a lowly 112th position among 190 countries, and even though USA not in the Top 10 ranks according to a 2012 World Health Organization (WHO) report.

Features

Hospital

This feature assists users to find out the nearest hospital, the app auto search location between 2 to 4 km radius during day and 5to 10 km during the night. Applications find out nearest hospitals and show in the map. Select the hospital - it shows the contact number and address. Call directly to book appointment or ask availability of Doctor.

Medical

This feature assists users to find out the nearest Medical store, the app auto search location between 2 to 4 km radius during day and 5to 10 km during the night. Applications find out nearest Medical stores and show in the map. Select the medical store - it shows the contact number and address. Call directly to check for the availability of medicines also Medical stores should also show if Delivery option is available.

Pathology lab

This feature assists users to find out the nearest Pathology lab, the app auto search location between 2 to 4 km radius during day and 5to 10 km during the night. Applications find out nearest pathology lab and show in the map. Select the Pathology lab - it shows the contact number and address. Call directly to check for the availability of test also pathology should also show if Door to Door service option availability.

Blood Bank

This feature assists users to find out the nearest Blood bank, the app auto search location between 2 to 4 km radius during day and 5to 10 km during the night. Applications find out nearest blood bank and show in the map. Select the Blood bank - it shows the contact number and address. Call directly to check for the availability of Blood also Blood Bank should also show if Door to Door service option availability.

Blood Donor

This feature assists users to find out the nearest Blood donor. Generate a request by filling an e form. App uses the algorithm and within the radius of 5-10 km by filtering all the parameters, sends a request to all the regist blood donors and blood banks. if the donors agrees to donate blood, then he shall authorize the app to share his details with the request genarator (receiver). There can be direct communication between donor and receiver. After the process is complete, both close the request in app with necessary details like OTP. Pint points can be credited to the donor.

Organ Donation

This feature assists users to assist in organ donation. Generate a request by filling an e form. App uses the algorithm and within the radius of 5-10 km by filtering all the parameters, sends a request to registered hospital. Authorised Hospital representative will direct communicate to donar for needful process. After the process is complete, both close the request in app with necessary details by OTP. E-card points may be credited to the donor.

Ambulance

This feature assists users to find out the nearest Offline & Online information for Ambulance and contact details during emergency.

Medical & Medicine Directory

This feature assists users to find out Offline information for definition /diseases / symptom / causes / treatment Information of drugs purpose / dosage / side effect

Helpline Numbers

This feature assists users to find out Information of all major help line numbers related medical field including medical financial help, child help line, and list of various government schemes.

Major announcement

This feature assists users to find out Information for major announcement or events from government entity, natural or manmade disaster news coverage, blood camp organisation details or any social events etc.

Health tips

This feature assists users to find out his own health analysis, It has got over health tools like BMI, Heart rate calculator etc.

OTP

Generate OTP is the most important part for safety and to avail the information. in the case of blood donation, it will eliminate the unnecessary and prank cases. Similarly, if any one donates blood at blood bank, blood bank provides OTP and it is credited to blood donor account so whenever blood donor needs blood he can use his Pint point.
